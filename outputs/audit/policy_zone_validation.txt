POLICY ZONE VALIDATION AUDIT
============================

Running the aggregate_histogram on the ingested shapefile asset yields:
print(mergedVillages.aggregate_histogram('Policy_Zon'));

Results:
- Policy Failure Zone: 0
- Intervention Success Zone: 0
- Biomass Procurement Zone: 0
- Baseline Zone: 0
- Unclassified: 12,467

Conclusion:
The `Policy_Zon` values were lost during the shapefile creation/ingestion process.
All 12,467 villages currently read as "Unclassified".
This explains why the Priority Index calculation is not applying the Policy Score correctly. The root cause needs to be fixed at the data pipeline level before re-ingesting the shapefile to Earth Engine.
